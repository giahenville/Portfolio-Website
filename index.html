Hi, I’m Gia, a student and software engineer. One word I’d use to describe myself is curious. More than anything else, I love learning new things. I’m constantly looking for unique opportunities to challenge myself and make new connections. I’ve participated in numerous programs to learn more about the technology industry and explore the paths that interest me most.


I want to be a software engineer because technology plays such an important role in our lives, and I want to understand how it all works. Growing up, I saw technology as some kind of magic. Now that I have the opportunity to learn and understand how things work, and knowing that this ‘magic’ is something I too can create, my interest has only grown. My goal is to keep learning and growing, and making contributions that make a real difference.


Beyond academics, I love to paint, crochet, play web games, and read. Thank you for visiting my webpage!
